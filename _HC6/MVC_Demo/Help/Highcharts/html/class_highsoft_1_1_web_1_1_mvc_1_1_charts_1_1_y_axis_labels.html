YAxisLabelsPosition3d Highsoft.Web.Mvc.Charts.YAxisLabels.Position3d
getset

Defines how the labels are be repositioned according to the 3D chartorientation.

  • &#39;offset&#39;: Maintain a fixed horizontal/vertical distance from the tick marks, despite the chart orientation. This is the backwards compatible behavior, and causes skewing of X and Z axes.
  • &#39;chart&#39;: Preserve 3D position relative to the chart.This looks nice, but hard to read if the text isn&#39;tforward-facing.
  • &#39;flap&#39;: Rotated text along the axis to compensate for the chart orientation. This tries to maintain text as legible as possible on all orientations.
  • &#39;ortho&#39;: Rotated text along the axis direction so that the labels are orthogonal to the axis. This is very similar to &#39;flap&#39;, but prevents skewing the labels (X and Y scaling are still present).
